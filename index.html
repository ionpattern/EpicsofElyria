About Navesi

I have been playing in text-based games (MUDs) for over 20 years, and they inspired me to put story first and foremost.  As a result, I've created dozens of original works in a number of styles.  My favorite and most common works are short stories and newspaper articles, but lately I've decided to challenge myself with some difficult styles of poetry, and I now mark major events in my characters' stories with ballads or epic poems.

I'm looking for a new challenge: writing for you.  I hope this will allow me to keep learning and pushing myself, and also provide you with something you can cherish and display.

Q&A

Do you accept cash?

No, only EP or items.  Part of what makes playing a game fun for me is finding creative ways to get ahead without cash, and earning items and EP through my work would allow me to do that.

How long does it take to write something?

I'm doing this work for a game, so it'll always happen in my spare time.  It could take anywhere from 1 day to several weeks, depending on the strictness of the style, the length, research needed, and whether there are others ahead of you.  I'll do my best to communicate with you an estimate, but with creative works the confidence window is not always perfect.
