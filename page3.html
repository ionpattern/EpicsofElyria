An enormous crystal shard cut to engulf the hand.
This gigantic, hexagonal quartz crystal presents the rough perfection of geometry broken from ancient, cavernous growth. Its transparent clarity is at war with gulfs of cloudy inclusions, and its facets are coarse and lined, fitted together with the lopsided habit of minerals yet occasionally sharp with planar edges.  The interior is hollowed in a fluid, helical way that suggests a hand could slip inside to hold it, the shard enveloping hand and forearm as if growing directly from the wielder.
A sculpted white mistglass disc shaped into a resonant waveform.
The amorphous form of the disc is richly undulant, deep ripples connected in wobbly concentricity across the surface.  It resembles nothing more than a milky liquid reverberant with a sudden, thunderous sound.  Five tiny finger holes in the back are placed such that the figure can be held upright, displayed in front of the hand as if emanating from the palm.
A glittering naga earcuff cut from a single dragon fire opal.
The naga's form is that of a sinuously curling serpent, designed so that when worn its body wraps seductively about the helix of the ear, its head resting near the concha as if whispering secrets to its wearer.  The translucent white opal sparkles in a rainbow of hues as it diffracts the ambient light, a single, brilliant flame set at its heart, snaking through the naga as though burning it from within.
A turbulent painting depicting the battle with the demon-possessed Ciriasa.

A deeply bearded windsteel axe embellished with gusts of icesteel.
The pure white spiritwood haft is wrapped thoroughly at the grip with pale ice-veined leather, the supple material cool to the touch, and the graceful line of the shaft supports a bearded blade that extends downward with a gentle arc.  The windsteel is flush with swirling lines that tumble over one another like blustery gales across a dark sky, while glimmering icesteel inlay enhances the blasting currents, adding streaks of snow and ice to the storm.

A layered dark steel armguard ablaze with icesteel and chromatic firestaining.
Overlapping layers of near-black steel form a distinctly scaled covering for the outer forearm.  Delicate icesteel inlay swirls across the surface like gusting winds of snow, in concert with the brilliant firestaining of the steel, which whorls in bright hues of orange and red.  Together the fire and ice dance in a conflagration of contrasting elements, tumultuously wending through one another with violent intensity.
